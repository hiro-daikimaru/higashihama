What is Ako Dantsu?

A handwoven cotton rug tradition from the castle town of Ako.

Ako Dantsu is counted alongside Nabeshima Dantsu and Sakai Dantsu as one of Japan's three major dantsu rug traditions. Made with cotton thread and patient handweaving, it has been passed down over many generations. Its quiet patterns, subtle colors, and tactile depth reveal a craft shaped by time.
